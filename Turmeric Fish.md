# Turmeric Fish

## Ingredients & Instructions

- 1 T sour cream
- 2.5 T fish sauce
- 1 T oil
- 1 T sugar
- 2 cloves garlic
- 2 tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp curry powder
- 1/2 tsp dill

Grind the garlic or chop it fine.  Mix all ingredients to make a marinade.

- 1 lb fish
- dill

Chop the fish into medium chunks.  Marinade for about an hour, then fry until
just done.  Add more dill if desired.  Serve over pasta or rice.
