# Brown Sugar Nutmeg Snaps

Makes about three dozen cookies.

## Ingredients & Instructions

- 1 3/4 C flour
- 1 tsp ground nutmeg
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1/2 tsp salt

Mix the dry ingredients in a small bowl.

- 1 1/4 C brown sugar
- 1/2 C butter, room temperature
- 1 egg

Beat the  butter and brown sugar  in a large bowl until fluffy.  Mix in the egg.
Add the dry ingredients and beat until well blended.

- sugar

Roll dough into one-inch balls.  Roll the bals in sugar to coat.  Place balls on
baking sheets, about two inches apart.  Use the bottom of a glass to flatten the
balls into into two-inch disks.  Bake at 350 F until golden, about 13 minutes.
